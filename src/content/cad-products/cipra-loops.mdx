There are sixteen 2"x2" Cipra tiles, each with a different configuration of paths. Put them together and they create loops on the surface of a torus. The smallest loops pass through four tiles. Can you arrange the tiles so that every loop passes through just four squares? Can you arrange them so that there is just one loop? Create your own challenges, and explore the many interesting properties of these tiles. Learn more atour blog post about Cipra tiles. Kit includes 16 tiles, booklet, and muslin carrying bag.
