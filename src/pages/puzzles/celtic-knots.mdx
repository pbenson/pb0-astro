# Celtic Knots

Build Celtic knot patterns by placing tiles on a grid. Each tile connects points on its edges, and tiles must match where they meet.

## How to Play

- Green cells show where tiles can be placed
- Click a green cell to see available tiles
- Click a tile in the popup to place it
- Corner tiles are pre-placed and fixed
